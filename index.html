Our Story

Bridging Education &
Industry-Ready Skills

The Technical Training Center Hub began as a small initiative with a big vision: to bridge the gap between traditional education and industry-ready skills. Founded by educators and industry experts, the center started with workshops in welding, electrical work, and automotive repair.

Word quickly spread as students gained not only technical expertise but also the confidence to pursue meaningful careers. Over the years, the hub expanded to include modern fields like CNC machining, HVACR, web design, graphic design, culinary arts, and supply chain logistics.

Today, the hub stands as a beacon for practical education in Balochistan — where innovation meets tradition, empowering individuals with certifications and real-world experience they need to thrive.

Our Partners

Backed by Strong Institutions

Government bodies and leading organizations supporting quality technical education in Balochistan.

Primary Donor
Government of Balochistan

Serves as the primary donor and supporter of TTC Hub, providing financial resources, infrastructure, and policy support. Focuses on inclusivity for women, marginalized groups, and unemployed youth.

Adopted By
SZABIST ZABTech (iTVE)

A leading vocational and technical education network under SZABIST. Provides demand-driven training to youth in underdeveloped regions, offering career counseling, internships, and job placement support.

Monitoring Agency
B-TEVTA

Balochistan Technical Education and Vocational Training Authority ensures quality standards, updated curricula, instructor training, and inclusive opportunities across the province.

Implementing Partner
BPPPA

Balochistan Public Private Partnership Authority facilitates project design, regulatory compliance, and promotes transparency in execution — bridging government and private sector for sustainable development.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

🎯 Mission

To prioritize the development of a demand-driven, dynamic, and integrated technical education and vocational training service that contributes to economic growth, innovation, and global competitiveness.

🌟 Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in technical and vocational education, empowering individuals with innovative skills and knowledge to contribute effectively to the economic and social development of Pakistan.

⚖️ Values

TTC Hub values excellence, practical skills, and integrity while promoting innovation, discipline, and continuous learning. We foster collaboration, ensure safety, and prepare students for successful careers.
