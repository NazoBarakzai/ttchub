Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

📅 Effective: June 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 18, 2026
This policy applies to TTC Hub (Technical Training Center Hub), Hub Chowki Road, Hub, Balochistan, managed with SZABIST ZABTech (iTVE). By using our website or submitting an application, you agree to these practices.

01 Information We Collect

  • Full name & phone — to contact you about your application
  • Email & age — optional; to verify eligibility and send updates
  • Preferred trade & message — to match you with the right program
  • Anonymous analytics — browser type, pages visited (no personal identifiers)
We do not collect ID numbers, financial details, or medical records.

02 How We Use Your Information

  • Process and review your course application
  • Contact you to confirm enrollment or share updates
  • Maintain records required by B-TEVTA and SZABIST ZABTech
  • Generate anonymized statistics for government reporting
We never use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

03 Sharing Your Information

Your data may only be shared with:

  • B-TEVTA & SZABIST ZABTech — for certification and compliance
  • Government of Balochistan / BPPPA — anonymized reports under funding agreements
  • Employers — only with your explicit consent, for job placement
  • Legal authorities — if required by Pakistani law

04 Security & Retention

Access is restricted to relevant staff. We use encrypted channels (HTTPS, WhatsApp).

  • Enrolled student records kept for 5 years after completion
  • Unsuccessful applicant data deleted after 1 year
  • Analytics data purged after 12 months

05 Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your data at any time, or withdraw consent for non-essential communications. We respond within 14 working days.

06 Students Under 18

Applicants under 18 require parental consent before data is processed. Contact us immediately if you believe your child submitted data without your permission.

07 Cookies, Links & Updates

Cookies: Essential and anonymous analytics only — no advertising or tracking cookies.

External links: We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites we link to.

Updates: Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use means acceptance.

Questions? Contact Us

We respond to all privacy requests within 14 working days.
